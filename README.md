## 🏠 What this app does

family-doctor is an AI family health companion for daily care at home. It helps you track vitals, keep medical notes, manage long-term conditions, and stay organized across your whole household.

It brings 25 health skills into one app, so you can handle common family health tasks in one place. You can use it to:

- Track blood pressure, pulse, weight, and temperature
- Store health records for each family member
- Follow care plans for long-term conditions
- Log mood, sleep, food, and exercise
- Keep reminders for checkups, meds, and tests
- Review trends over time with simple charts and reports
